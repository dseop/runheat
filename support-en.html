RunHeat Support & Privacy Policy

About RunHeat

RunHeat is a heatmap-based running viewer app that visualizes your running workouts stored in the Apple Health app. It allows you to explore your running history through an interactive map-based heatmap.

Privacy Policy

RunHeat does not require account registration or login.

RunHeat does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The app accesses running workout data stored in the Apple Health app solely to generate heatmap visualizations on the user's device. All data processing occurs locally on the device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

RunHeat does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

Health Data Access

RunHeat may request permission to access running workout data from Apple Health in order to generate heatmaps.

This permission is used strictly for providing core functionality. Health data is processed only on-device and is not transmitted externally.

Data Retention

RunHeat does not operate any backend servers and does not store personal data. All data processing remains on the user's device.
